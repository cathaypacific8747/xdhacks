About us
Who are we?
We are Team 5 from CKY XdHacks, an online 2-day hackathon focused on creativity, technology and entrepreneurship.
Parcitipants have been asked to present a solution that solves education issues, by developing a brand and a project using their programming skills. Competitors will then present their project to a qualified panel of professionals in the technology field, who will decide on the winner based on the projects effectiveness, value, design, innovation, feasibility and presentation.
We recieved the Best Presentation Award, and after one month of further development, we are happy to introduce our final end product to everyone.

Our Vision
Books have long been an essential part of education in our school:
"Our school has been promoting the use of readers and novels as the main source of materials for teaching languages."
- Principal Lau
The systemic issue in the world of literacy is that books are expensive.
We believe that money should not be a barrier to education.
In the fast paced modern lifestyle, literacy culture is declining.
As a result of this, independent bookstores are vanishing, and are replaced by the mass commercialisation of literature in the form of chain bookstores.
We have therefore created this second-hand book selling platform with the belief that books should be accessible, equitable and affordable.
What are the benefits?
Swappy was built specifically for books only and for CKY students.
Data privacy and Trust
While the Internet is becoming increasingly convenient, your data are often sold and transferred to third-party companies and advertisers, breaching your privacy.
As students of CKY, we are committed to protect your data.
Swappy warns users accordingly if the buyer of seller isn't from CKY, adding a layer of trust (this is done by detecting whether the user's e-mail address belongs to CKY, which cannot be changed after the user has signed up).
By default, your contact information will be private. This means that no one will be able to access it, until it you manually disclose the information to the buyer/seller, or set it to public.
We will also never share your information to any third-party unless required by law.
Security have always been our top priority, with protections including but not limited to resistance against SQL injection, XSS attacks and CSRF.
You can read more about your data in our privacy notice.
Convenient and Localised
While second-hand trading platforms such as eBay and Carousell offer book exchanges, they can be rather time-consuming and logistically challenging to collate or sell multiple books.
With Swappy, you can choose to exchange books at CKY, eliminating the need to transport heavy books to/from your home.
Resaleability
Comparing to local textbook stores, the resale rates can be much higher because there is no need for storage fees.
Affordability
Comparing to traditional auctioning platforms such as Amazon, these platforms often earn a large commission and often comes with many hidden delivery fees.
Swappy is entirely free and will never collect any funds, as payments will be performed on a mutual agreement between the buyer and the seller.
To learn how this platform works, please visit our help page.
